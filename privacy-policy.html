隐私政策

生效日期：2026年7月15日  |  最近更新：2026年7月18日

欢迎使用 Story Buddy（故事伙伴）（以下简称"本应用"）。本应用是由 aSphere（以下简称"我们"）提供的、面向儿童阅读与教育场景的人工智能语音互动助手。我们高度重视用户尤其是未成年人的个人信息与隐私安全。本隐私政策旨在向您说明我们如何收集、使用、存储、共享和保护相关信息，以及您所享有的权利。

本应用主要面向中国大陆用户，并同时服务香港、澳门等地区用户。我们依据《中华人民共和国个人信息保护法》《中华人民共和国网络安全法》《中华人民共和国数据安全法》《未成年人保护法》《儿童个人信息网络保护规定》等法律法规制定本政策。

请特别注意：本应用由人工智能提供服务。您在与本应用中的语音角色对话时，对话对象是人工智能程序，而非真实自然人。语音角色（如 Cherry、Ethan 等）均为虚拟形象，不具有真实人格，也不能替代现实中的亲情、友情或专业帮助。请理性使用，避免过度依赖。

在使用本应用前，请您（若使用者为未成年人，则为其父母或其他监护人）仔细阅读并充分理解本政策，特别是以加粗方式标识的条款。您点击同意或开始使用本应用，即表示您已阅读并同意本政策的全部内容。如您不同意，请停止使用本应用。

一、我们收集的信息

我们仅在为实现具体功能所必需的范围内，遵循合法、正当、必要和诚信原则收集信息。

1.1 账户信息

当父母或监护人注册账户时，我们收集：

  • 手机号码或电子邮箱（用于注册、登录与账户找回）；
  • 用户名与密码（密码经加密存储）；
  • 孩子的昵称、年龄（或年龄段）、性别（用于个性化适龄内容，属于儿童个人信息，处理前须取得监护人同意）；
  • 为响应极端情境干预需要，可收集监护人或紧急联系人的联系方式（见第五节）。

一键登录：若您使用运营商"本机号码一键登录"，我们及提供号码认证服务的合作方（阿里云）将在获得您勾选同意后，通过运营商网关获取本机号码用于登录与注册。相关处理还受《号码认证服务隐私权政策》约束。

1.2 语音与音频信息

语音互动是本应用的核心功能。当孩子使用本应用时：

  • 设备麦克风采集的音频将实时传输至我们的服务器，用于语音识别与人工智能应答生成；
  • 音频数据在会话过程中被即时处理；除为实现"高光时刻"等功能所必需外，非必要不在服务器上保存原始音频；
  • 语音属于敏感个人信息，我们仅将其用于提供本应用的语音互动功能，不用于其他目的。

1.3 摄像头与图像信息

"实景共读（Live Reading）"功能会使用设备摄像头拍摄绘本页面，以提供人工智能阅读辅助：

  • 图像被传输至服务器用于文字识别与内容分析；
  • 图像即时处理，处理完成后不长期保存。

1.4 对话与使用信息

  • 对话文本记录（语音互动转写的文字）可能被保留，用于在会话中维持上下文、生成阅读进度报告，以及提升服务质量；
  • 阅读统计信息，如使用时长、会话次数、阅读活动概要等。

1.5 设备与技术信息

我们可能自动收集：

  • 设备型号、操作系统版本、应用版本；
  • 语言与地区偏好；
  • 一般性使用分析（如功能使用频率）与必要的日志信息。

我们不会收集精确地理位置、通讯录，也不会为广告目的收集设备标识符。

二、我们如何使用信息

使用目的所用信息
提供人工智能阅读辅助与语音互动音频、图像、对话信息
按孩子的年龄与水平个性化内容与应答账户信息（年龄、性别）
展示阅读统计与成长进度使用信息、会话记录
用户认证与会话维持账户凭证、JWT 令牌

识别极端情境并进行必要干预对话文本、监护人/紧急联系人信息
提升服务质量、排查故障技术信息、使用分析

如需将您的信息用于本政策未载明的其他目的，我们将再次征得您的同意。

三、人工智能拟人化互动特别说明

就本应用的人工智能拟人化互动服务，我们作出如下说明与承诺：

  • AI 身份标识：我们通过显著方式提示用户正在与人工智能服务而非自然人进行互动，并对人工智能生成合成内容依法进行标识。
  • 不以替代社交为目标：本应用旨在辅助儿童阅读与语言发展，不以替代真实社会交往、控制用户心理或诱导沉迷依赖为目的。我们不向未成年人提供虚拟亲属、虚拟伴侣等虚拟亲密关系服务。
  • 情感边界与健康保护：我们采取措施避免过度迎合用户、诱导情感依赖，具备情感边界引导与心理健康保护能力。
  • 防沉迷提醒：发现用户出现过度依赖、沉迷倾向的，我们将以弹窗等显著方式动态提醒；对连续使用每超过 2 小时的，我们将以对话或弹窗等方式提醒用户注意使用时长。
  • 便捷退出：用户可通过界面操作、语音指令或关键词等方式随时退出，我们将立即停止服务，不以持续互动等方式阻碍退出。

四、未成年人保护

本应用主要面向儿童设计，我们对未成年人个人信息给予最严格的保护。

  • 监护人同意：账户须由父母或其他监护人创建。处理不满十四周岁儿童个人信息、以及向不满十四周岁未成年人提供拟人化互动服务的，我们将取得其父母或其他监护人的同意。
  • 身份识别与未成年人模式：我们采取有效措施识别未成年人用户；识别为未成年人的，将切换至未成年人模式，并提供模式切换、定期现实提醒、使用时长限制等个性化安全设置。
  • 监护人管理权：监护人可接收安全风险提醒、了解未成年人的服务使用概况、屏蔽特定角色、限制充值消费，并可随时查询、更正、删除孩子的信息或注销账户。
  • 禁止虚拟亲密关系：我们不向未成年人提供虚拟亲属、虚拟伴侣等虚拟亲密关系服务。
  • 适龄与内容安全：所有人工智能生成内容均经实时安全过滤、偏见检测与适龄可读性评估，避免生成可能引发未成年人模仿不安全行为或产生极端情绪的内容。
  • 最小必要与无定向广告：我们仅收集提供服务所必需的最少信息，不将未成年人信息用于任何定向或行为广告，本应用不含任何第三方广告。
  • 合规审计：我们对处理未成年人个人信息的活动定期开展合规审计。

五、极端情境的识别与干预

为保护用户的生命健康，当我们识别到用户可能出现极端情绪，或存在自残、自杀、遭受重大损失等威胁生命健康的极端情境时，我们将：

  • 生成情绪安抚、鼓励其寻求帮助的相关内容；
  • 在必要时提供相应求助渠道与援助信息；
  • 在必要时及时联络用户的监护人或紧急联系人。为此我们可能使用您在账户中提供的监护人/紧急联系人联系方式。

六、信息的委托处理、共享与转移

我们不会出售、出租您的个人信息。除本政策载明或取得您单独同意外，我们不会向第三方提供您的个人信息。仅在下列情形共享：

  • 委托处理（服务提供方）：我们使用第三方云服务处理语音识别、语音合成与人工智能语言模型请求。这些服务方仅按我们的指示、为实现本应用功能而处理数据，并受合同约束负有保密与安全义务。当前主要为阿里云（语音识别/语音合成/大模型/号码认证与短信服务）。
  • 交互数据保护：除法律另有规定或您明确同意外，我们不向第三方提供用户交互数据；除法律、行政法规另有规定或取得您单独同意外，我们不会将属于敏感个人信息的交互数据用于模型训练。
  • 法律要求：依据法律法规、法律程序或有权机关的要求而必须披露时。
  • 安全保护：为保护未成年人或公众的人身、财产安全所必需时。

七、信息的存储与安全

  • 我们在中华人民共和国境内收集和产生的个人信息，存储于境内服务器；如确需向境外提供，我们将依法履行相应义务（包括必要的安全评估或取得单独同意）。
  • 账户数据存储于安全服务器，采用传输加密（TLS/SSL）与存储加密。
  • 音频与图像即时处理，会话结束后不长期保存。
  • 认证通过 JSON Web Token（JWT）管理，具备自动过期与刷新机制。
  • 我们采取加密、访问控制等行业标准安全措施，防止未经授权的访问、篡改、披露或损毁。若发生个人信息安全事件，我们将依法及时告知并向主管部门报告。

八、信息的保存期限

  • 账户数据：在账户存续期间保存；账户注销后，相关个人信息将被删除或匿名化处理。
  • 对话历史：用于维持上下文与生成阅读进度报告，可应请求删除。
  • 音频与图像：即时处理，非必要不在服务器上保存。
  • 使用分析：以聚合、匿名化形式保存用于服务改进。

超出上述期限或法律法规要求的期限后，我们将删除或匿名化处理相关信息。

九、您的权利

在法律法规规定的范围内，您（或未成年人的监护人）就其个人信息享有以下权利：

  • 查阅与复制：获取我们持有的关于您或孩子的个人信息副本；
  • 更正与补充：更正不准确的个人信息；
  • 删除：请求删除个人信息与账户信息；
  • 撤回同意：随时撤回您所作出的同意（这可能影响相关功能的正常使用）；
  • 可携带：在符合条件时，请求以结构化、通用格式转移您的个人信息；
  • 注销账户：随时注销账户。

行使上述权利，或对个人信息处理有任何疑问、投诉，请通过第十一节的联系方式与我们联系。我们将在法定期限内响应。

十、投诉与举报机制

我们建立了便捷的申诉、投诉与举报机制。您可通过第十一节所列联系方式，就本应用的内容、服务或个人信息处理进行申诉、投诉或举报。我们将明确受理流程并在合理时限内予以处理和反馈。若您认为我们未妥善处理您的关切，您有权向网信、市场监管等主管部门反映，或依法寻求救济。

十二、政策的更新

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 15, 2026  |  Last Updated: July 18, 2026

Welcome to Story Buddy (the "App"), an AI-powered voice conversation assistant for children's reading and educational content, provided by aSphere ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting the privacy and safety of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and safeguard information, and the rights you have.

The App primarily serves users in Mainland China and also serves users in Hong Kong, Macau and other regions. This policy is made in accordance with the Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL), Cybersecurity Law, Data Security Law, Law on the Protection of Minors, and the Provisions on the Protection of Children's Personal Information Online, among other applicable laws of the People's Republic of China.

Important: This App is powered by artificial intelligence. When you talk to a voice character in the App, you are interacting with an AI program, not a real human being. The voice characters (e.g., Cherry, Ethan) are virtual and have no real personality; they cannot replace real family, friendship, or professional help. Please use the App reasonably and avoid over-reliance.

Before using the App, you (or, if the user is a minor, their parent or guardian) should carefully read and fully understand this policy, especially the clauses in bold. By clicking to agree or beginning to use the App, you confirm that you have read and agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please stop using the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information only within the scope necessary to provide specific features, following the principles of legality, legitimacy, necessity, and good faith.

1.1 Account Information

  • Mobile number or email address (for registration, login, and account recovery);
  • Username and password (passwords are stored encrypted);
  • The child's display name, age (or age range), and gender (to personalize age-appropriate content; this is children's personal information and requires guardian consent);
  • Where needed for extreme-situation intervention, contact details of a guardian or emergency contact (see Section 5).

One-tap login: If you use carrier "one-tap number login", we and our number-authentication partner (Alibaba Cloud) will, subject to your checkbox consent, obtain your device phone number via the carrier gateway for login and registration. This is also governed by the Number Authentication Service Privacy Policy.

1.2 Voice and Audio Data

  • Audio captured by the microphone is streamed to our servers in real time for speech recognition and AI response generation;
  • Audio is processed transiently; except where necessary for features such as "highlight moments", we do not store raw audio on our servers unless necessary;
  • Voice is sensitive personal information; we use it only to provide the App's voice-interaction feature.

1.3 Camera and Image Data

  • The Live Reading feature uses the camera to capture book pages; images are transmitted for text recognition and content analysis;
  • Images are processed transiently and not stored permanently after processing.

1.4 Usage and Conversation Data

  • Conversation transcripts may be retained to maintain in-session context, produce reading-progress reports, and improve service quality;
  • Reading statistics such as session duration, number of sessions, and activity summaries.

1.5 Device and Technical Information

  • Device type, OS version, and app version; language/locale preferences; general usage analytics and necessary logs.

We do not collect precise geolocation or contacts, and do not collect device identifiers for advertising.

3. AI Anthropomorphic Interaction Notice

Regarding the App's AI anthropomorphic interaction service, we state and undertake as follows:

  • AI identification: We conspicuously indicate that you are interacting with an AI service rather than a natural person, and we label AI-generated content as required by law.
  • Not a substitute for social interaction: The App is designed to assist children's reading and language development, and is not intended to replace real social interaction, control users' psychology, or induce addiction/dependence. We do not provide virtual kinship or virtual companion (intimate-relationship) services to minors.
  • Emotional boundaries and mental-health protection: We take measures to avoid excessive pandering or inducing emotional dependence, with emotional-boundary guidance and mental-health protection capabilities.
  • Anti-addiction reminders: If we detect signs of over-reliance or addiction, we will dynamically remind users via conspicuous means such as pop-ups; and for every 2 hours of continuous use, we will remind users to be mindful of usage time via dialogue or pop-up.
  • Easy exit: Users may exit at any time via UI actions, voice commands, or keywords; we will stop the service immediately and will not obstruct exit.

4. Protection of Minors

  • Guardian consent: Accounts must be created by a parent or guardian. For processing the personal information of, and providing anthropomorphic interaction services to, children under 14, we obtain the consent of their parent or other guardian.
  • Identification & Minor Mode: We take effective measures to identify minor users; identified minors are switched to a Minor Mode offering mode switching, periodic reality reminders, and usage-time limits.
  • Guardian controls: Guardians can receive safety-risk alerts, review the minor's usage overview, block specific characters, restrict top-ups/spending, and access, correct, or delete the child's information or close the account at any time.
  • No virtual intimate relationships: We do not provide virtual kinship or virtual companion services to minors.
  • Age-appropriate & content safety: All AI-generated content is evaluated in real time via safety filtering, bias detection, and age-appropriate readability checks, avoiding content that could induce unsafe imitation or extreme emotions in minors.
  • Data minimization & no targeted ads: We collect only the minimum necessary information and never use minors' data for targeted or behavioral advertising; the App contains no third-party ads.
  • Compliance audit: We periodically conduct compliance audits of our processing of minors' personal information.

5. Identification and Intervention in Extreme Situations

To protect users' life and health, when we identify possible extreme emotions or extreme situations threatening life and health (e.g., self-harm, suicide, or major loss), we will: generate soothing content encouraging the user to seek help; provide help channels and assistance information where necessary; and, where necessary, promptly contact the user's guardian or emergency contact using the details provided in the account.

6. Entrusted Processing, Sharing, and Transfer

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Except as stated in this policy or with your separate consent, we do not provide your personal information to third parties. We share only:

  • Entrusted processing (service providers): We use third-party cloud services for speech recognition, text-to-speech, and AI language models, which process data only per our instructions and under confidentiality/security obligations. Currently this is mainly Alibaba Cloud (speech recognition/text-to-speech/LLM/number-authentication and SMS).
  • Interaction-data protection: Unless otherwise provided by law or with your explicit consent, we do not provide user interaction data to third parties; and unless otherwise provided by laws/regulations or with your separate consent, we do not use interaction data that constitutes sensitive personal information for model training.
  • Legal requirements and safety of minors or the public, where necessary.

7. Storage and Security

  • Personal information collected within Mainland China is stored on servers within China; any cross-border transfer will comply with applicable obligations (including security assessment or separate consent where required).
  • Account data is stored on secure servers with encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest;
  • Audio and images are processed transiently and not retained after the session;
  • Authentication is managed via JWT with automatic expiry/refresh;
  • We apply industry-standard measures such as encryption and access control and will report and notify security incidents as required by law.

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained while the account is active; deleted or anonymized upon account closure.
  • Conversation history: retained for context and progress reports; deletable on request.
  • Audio/images: processed in real time and not stored on our servers unless necessary.
  • Usage analytics: retained in aggregated, anonymized form.

9. Your Rights

You (or a minor's guardian) may exercise the rights to access/copy, correct/supplement, delete, withdraw consent, port your data, and close the account, to the extent provided by law. To exercise these rights or raise questions/complaints, contact us via Section 11; we will respond within statutory timeframes.

10. Complaints and Reporting

We maintain accessible channels for appeals, complaints, and reports. Using the contacts in Section 11, you may appeal, complain, or report regarding content, services, or personal-information processing. We will handle and respond within a reasonable time. If you believe your concern is not properly addressed, you may raise it with the competent authorities or seek legal remedies.
