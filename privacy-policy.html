Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 11, 2026  |  Last Updated: February 11, 2026

Welcome to Story Buddy, a product of aSphere ("we", "us", or "our"). Story Buddy is an AI-powered voice conversation assistant designed for children's reading and educational content. We are committed to protecting the privacy and safety of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application ("App") and related services.

By using Story Buddy, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When a parent or guardian creates an account, we collect:

  • Username and password
  • Child's age, gender, and display name (used to personalize the experience)

1.2 Voice and Audio Data

Story Buddy uses voice interaction as a core feature. When your child uses the App:

  • Audio is captured via the device microphone and streamed to our servers in real time for speech recognition and AI response generation.
  • Audio data is processed transiently and is not stored permanently on our servers after the conversation session ends.

1.3 Camera and Image Data

The Live Reading feature uses the device camera to capture images of book pages for AI-powered reading assistance:

  • Images are transmitted to our servers for text recognition and content analysis.
  • Images are processed transiently and are not stored permanently after processing is complete.

1.4 Usage and Conversation Data

  • Conversation transcripts (text-based records of voice interactions) may be retained to maintain conversation context within a session and to improve service quality.
  • Reading statistics such as session duration, number of sessions, and reading activity summaries.

1.5 Device and Technical Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Language and locale preferences
  • General usage analytics (e.g., feature usage frequency)

We do not collect precise geolocation data, contacts, or device identifiers for advertising purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Purpose Data Used
Provide AI-powered reading assistance and voice interaction Audio, images, conversation data
Personalize content and responses for the child's age and level Account information (age, gender)
Display reading statistics and progress Usage data, session records
Authenticate users and maintain sessions Account credentials, JWT tokens
Improve service quality and fix bugs Technical data, usage analytics
Ensure content safety through real-time evaluation Conversation transcripts

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Story Buddy is designed for use by children, and we take children's privacy seriously.

  • Parental Consent: Account creation requires a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.
  • Minimal Collection: We collect only the information necessary to provide the service. We do not collect more data from children than is reasonably needed.
  • No Behavioral Advertising: We do not use children's data for targeted or behavioral advertising. Story Buddy contains no third-party ads.
  • No Social Features: The App does not include social networking, public profiles, or user-to-user communication features.
  • Content Safety: All AI-generated content is evaluated in real time through safety filters, bias detection, and age-appropriate readability checks.
  • Parental Control: Parents or guardians can review their child's account information, request deletion of data, or close the account at any time by contacting us.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use third-party cloud services (e.g., Microsoft Azure) to process speech recognition, text-to-speech, and AI language model requests. These providers process data on our behalf and are contractually obligated to protect it.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Safety: We may disclose information if we believe it is necessary to protect the safety of a child or the public.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Account data is stored on secure servers with encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest.
  • Audio and image data are processed transiently and are not retained after the session.
  • Authentication is managed via JSON Web Tokens (JWT) with automatic expiry and refresh mechanisms.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for as long as the account is active. Upon account deletion, all associated data is permanently removed.
  • Conversation history is retained to maintain context and provide reading progress reports. It can be deleted upon request.
  • Audio and image data are processed in real time and are not permanently stored.
  • Usage analytics are retained in aggregated, anonymized form for service improvement.

7. Third-Party Services

Story Buddy integrates the following third-party services for core functionality:

  • Microsoft Azure Cognitive Services - Speech recognition, text-to-speech, and AI language models
  • OpenAI (via Azure) - AI language model for conversation and reading assistance

These services process data according to their respective privacy policies. We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or social media SDKs.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You (or the parent/guardian of a child user) have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you or your child.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of personal data and account information.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time (which may affect App functionality).
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, commonly used format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at email@asphere-ai.com.

9. Permissions Used

Story Buddy requires the following device permissions:

Permission Purpose
Microphone Voice interaction with the AI reading assistant
Camera Capturing book pages for the Live Reading feature

These permissions are requested only when the relevant feature is used and can be revoked at any time through your device settings.

10. International Data Transfers

Our servers and third-party service providers may be located outside your country of residence. By using Story Buddy, you consent to the transfer of your information to these locations. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for cross-border data transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.
