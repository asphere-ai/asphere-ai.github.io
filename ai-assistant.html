Why do books sometimes use a lot of hard words when they could just use simple ones? It makes it so much harder to read!
That's a great observation! Authors choose specific words for different reasons. Sometimes, they use more complex words because:
1. These words can express exactly what they want to say like using "sprinted" instead of "ran" tells us someone ran very fast
2. Different words help make the writing more interesting imagine eating your favorite food described only as "good" every time
